Mutton

Lamb, hogget, and mutton, generically sheep meat are the meat of domestic sheep. Mutton is the meat of a mature adult sheep, typically between two and three years old. Since the animal is older, it contains more fat and muscle, resulting in a stronger flavor and denser, tougher texture.

Paneer
